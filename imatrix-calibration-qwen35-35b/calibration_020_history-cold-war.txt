*   **1947: Truman Doctrine** – Announced U.S. policy of containment to stop Soviet expansion globally.
*   **1949: NATO Formation** – Established a unified Western military alliance against the Eastern Bloc.
*   **1950-1953: Korean War** – Became the first major armed proxy conflict between superpowers.
*   **1961: Berlin Wall Built** – Physically divided East and West Berlin, symbolizing the Iron Curtain.
*   **1962: Cuban Missile Crisis** – Pushed nuclear brinkmanship to its highest historical point.
*   **1972: SALT I Signed** – Marked a peak in détente with the first major nuclear arms limitation treaty.
*   **1979: Soviet Invasion of Afghanistan** – Ended détente and renewed intense global military tension.
*   **1985: Gorbachev Comes to Power** – Initiated reforms (glasnost/perestroika) that weakened central control.
*   **1989: Fall of the Berlin Wall** – Symbolized the collapse of communist regimes in Eastern Europe.
*   **1991: Dissolution of the USSR** – Officially ended the forty-year Cold War conflict.
